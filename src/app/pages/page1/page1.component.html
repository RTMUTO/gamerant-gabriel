Silent Hill 2 and Every Rumored Game For PlayStation's May Event Explained

PlayStation is rumored to be having an event in May to showcase upcoming games, including Silent Hill 2 along with a few other potential titles.

BY DREW SWANSON PUBLISHED APR 6, 2024

Silent Hill 2 Remake

The game that seems the most likely to appear at next month's rumored PlayStation event is the Silent Hill 2 remake being developed by Bloober Team. Recent leaked promotional material from GameStop and an ESRB rating for Silent Hill 2 appearing online suggest the game is approaching release, which lines up with statements made by reputable insider Jeff Grubb on the Giant Bombcast that the release date will be announced for Silent Hill 2 at this event in May.

Ghost of Tsushima 2

While the game hasn't been confirmed to be in development, the success of Sucker Punch's 2020 hit Ghost of Tsushima could mean a sequel is on its way. According to leaker Silknigth, Ghost of Tsushima 2 is set to be revealed in the coming months, potentially putting it in a prime position to be announced at May's PlayStation event. While it likely won't be released before April 2025, it would still be exciting to see what Sucker Punch has in store for this follow-up.

Concord

Originally announced at the PlayStation Showcase 2023 last May, Concord is a PvP multiplayer FPS from developer Firewalk Studios that could be another title shown at the rumored May PlayStation Showcase. The game was originally given a 2024 release window when it was first announced but has seen few details since, potentially teeing it up for a grand re-reveal at this year's PlayStation Showcase.
